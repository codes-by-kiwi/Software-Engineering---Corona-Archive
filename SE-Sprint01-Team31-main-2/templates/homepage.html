Corona Archive Home Page

Hello, this is the corona archive home page. This web service aims to provide digital tracking of citizens that enter certain
places and keeping the records in case of a covid infection spread. To proceed with our services, go to the login or the registration page.

Visit Us To Know More

Services We Offer

First Service

Exposure notifications - we tell if you have come into contact with a person with covid.

Second Service

We allow you to post your booster and vaccination status so that we can determine what date you should book your next appointment on.

Third Service

We tell you the necessary steps to take in order to avoid the spread of the disease and how to take care of yourself.
